About the Course

Course Summary

An Overview of the Students' Experience

  • Learning

    Introducing Students to Coding

    As this is one of the first CS courses that students at Illinois will have taken, we will begin by introducing them to various programming languages. They will practice their skills by working on Project Euler, and get used to writing programs.

  • Formulating Products

    And Making Companies

    Students will develop ideas for software products, and plan out models for how to go about developing them. They will pitch these ideas in front of the class, and after discussion, we will group students into companies in order to develop one of several products.

  • Divide and Conquer

    Developing Sections of the Product

    In each company, students will make sub-groups such as iOS, Android, Database, Web Design, and others. The groups will work on their portion of the project, making periodic reports to their company, under supervision of one of our course staff.

  • Bringing it Together

    Merging the Seperate Sections

    Once the sub-groups have finished their portions of the project, they will work together to merge their work. For example, they will integrate the frontend with the data, among other things. By doing so, they will create and industry grade software product and prepare to present.
